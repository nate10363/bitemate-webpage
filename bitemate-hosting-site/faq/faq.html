COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Our reason for existence

bitemate was created with a simple goal: to provide users with multiple ways to easily, effectively, and enjoyably explore all of the cuisines, and by extent cultures, that the world has to offer... from the traditional delicacies of the mountains of Tibet to the 4k recording equipment of your favorite local foodie creator

How can I join the party?

coming to an iOS and Google Play store near you, we will proudly feature the links once the creation of our platform is complete :)

add a payment method?

go to Settings, Edit payments, Add Card

log out or delete account?

go to Settings and choose either log out or delete account and enter prompts

forgot password when logging in?

click on Forgot password prompt below the Password boxes and fill in your email. you'll receive your one time password as an email titled 'Password Reset - Royal Pine'

change password?

go to Settings, Change Password, enter info
